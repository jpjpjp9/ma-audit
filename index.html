About This Diagnostic

This 5-minute diagnostic evaluates your organization across seven behavioral and operational dimensions. Research shows 83% of failed M&A deals cite poor integration as the root cause (Bain, 2023), and only 14% of companies achieve full strategic, operational, and financial success post-acquisition (PwC, 2023). The failure points are rarely financial - theyâ€™re human: misaligned leadership, cultural friction, and unclear decision paths. This tool helps surface those risks before they compromise value, slow growth, or derail the next exit.

Instructions: Rate each statement on a scale of 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 5 (Strongly Agree) based on your organization's current reality.

Scoring System

Question Weights:

  • CRITICAL (2x Weight) - Fundamental areas that predict M&A success/failure
  • HIGH IMPORTANCE (1.5x Weight) - Key structural elements for integration
  • STANDARD (1x Weight) - Important supporting factors

Automatic Fail Conditions:

  • Deal-Breakers: Any score of "1" (Strongly Disagree) on questions marked as Deal-Breakers will result in an automatic "NOT READY" assessment.
  • Red Flags: Any score of "1" on questions marked as Red Flags will require minimum 6-month remediation before M&A consideration.
